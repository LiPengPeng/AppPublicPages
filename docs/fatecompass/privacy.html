🔐 Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2024

Introduction

Fate ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide

  • Birth Information: Birth date, time, and location for astrological calculations
  • Profile Information: Nickname, relationship designation (for family/friend profiles)
  • Optional Contact Information: Email address (if you choose to contact support)

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device information (device model, OS version, app version)
  • App usage analytics and crash reports
  • Performance metrics for improvement purposes

How We Use Your Information

  • Calculate and provide astrological readings and predictions
  • Personalize your experience and recommendations
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Respond to your support inquiries
  • Send technical notifications about your account

Data Storage & Security

  • Local Storage: Birth information is stored locally on your device by default
  • Optional Cloud Sync: iCloud sync encrypts data end-to-end if enabled
  • No Data Selling: We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties
  • Anonymous Analytics: Analytics data is anonymized and aggregated for improvement

Your Privacy Rights

  • Access: You can view and export your data anytime
  • Delete: You can delete your profile and data from app settings
  • Control: You control which data is synced to cloud storage
  • Opt-out: You can disable analytics in app settings

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will delete such information promptly.

Policy Changes

We may update this policy occasionally to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes via the app or email if you've provided one.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the app's support feature or visit our support page.
