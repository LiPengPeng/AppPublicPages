💬 Support & Help

Getting Started

How do I set up my profile?

Open the "My" tab and tap "Complete My Birth Information". Enter your birth date, time, and location. If you're unsure about your birth time, you can estimate—you can always update it later.

What is Ba Zi?

Ba Zi (八字), also known as the Four Pillars of Destiny, is a Chinese metaphysical system that uses your birth date and time to analyze personality traits, fortune, and life patterns.

Can I add family members?

Yes! Go to the "My" tab and tap "Add Family / Partner / Friend". You can then view readings for their profiles.

Common Questions

Is my data stored securely?
Yes. Your birth information is stored locally on your device by default. If you enable iCloud sync, data is encrypted end-to-end. We never sell or share your personal data.
Can I delete my data?
Absolutely. Go to "My" tab → Profile settings → "Delete Account" to remove all your data from the app.
What if my birth time is uncertain?
Birth time is important for accuracy, but if you don't know it, you can estimate based on public records or ask family members. You can update it anytime in your profile settings.
Are these readings accurate?
Our readings are based on traditional Ba Zi and Five Elements theory. They're for entertainment and self-reflection purposes. For important life decisions, please consult professionals.
How do I report a bug?
You can report issues through the app's feedback feature or contact us directly using the form below.
Is the app available offline?
Most features work offline once data is loaded. However, some content like courses and guides require internet access.
How do I restore purchases?
Go to "My" tab → Settings → "Restore Purchases". This will restore any purchased features on your current device.
What's the difference between free and premium readings?
Free readings include basic fortune insights and daily readings. Premium readings unlock deeper analysis including full personal reports, yearly forecasts, and compatibility readings.

Troubleshooting

App Crashes

If the app crashes:

  • Make sure you have the latest version from App Store
  • Restart your device
  • Force close the app and reopen it
  • Clear app cache in Settings → General → iPhone Storage

Readings Not Loading

If readings won't load:

  • Check your internet connection
  • Verify your birth information is complete and accurate
  • Try restarting the app
  • Log out and log back in if you have an account

Data Not Syncing

If iCloud sync isn't working:

  • Verify iCloud is enabled in Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud
  • Check internet connectivity
  • Sign out and back into iCloud
  • Disable and re-enable sync in app settings
